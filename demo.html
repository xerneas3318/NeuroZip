bpp = bits per EEG sample. One epoch is 63×250 = 15,750 samples.
↓ lower bpp = more compressed (better storage).
↑ higher retrieval % = more task info kept (better quality).
Goal: low bpp + high retrieval. Raw EEG @ 16 bpp (fp16) is the upper bound.
